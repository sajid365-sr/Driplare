# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Status:** [ COMPLIANT ]

---

### 1. Data Collection & Intent
We collect information strictly necessary to engineer your systems. This includes:
* **Identification Data:** Name, email, and company details provided via our Intake Suite.
* **Technical Data:** API keys or database credentials provided by you for integration purposes (Stored via AES-256 encryption).
* **Usage Data:** How you interact with our AI agents to improve model response accuracy.

### 2. Data Sovereignty (The AI Clause)
Unlike many "wrapper" services, Driplare prioritizes your data privacy:
* **No Model Training:** We do not use your proprietary business data to train public LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.).
* **RAG Isolation:** Your private knowledge base is stored in isolated vector databases.

### 3. Third-Party Integrations
Our services often involve n8n, MongoDB, and OpenAI. While we engineer the "bridge," your data is subject to the privacy policies of these providers once it leaves the Driplare ecosystem.

### 4. Data Retention
We retain project-related data only for the duration of the contract plus 90 days for emergency recovery, unless otherwise requested in writing.

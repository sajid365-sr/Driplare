# Page Blueprint: The Intake Suite
**Concept:** Where Humans Meet Intelligence.

---

## 1. Hero Section: The Executive Greeting
**Component:** `ContactHero`
**Style:** Large, clean typography. No distractions.

* **Headline:** Let’s Engineer Your Next **Competitive Advantage.**
* **Sub-headline:** Choose your preferred path: Talk to our AI Architect Assistant for instant scheduling, or send a manual brief.

---

## 2. The AI Concierge (The Innovation Zone)
**Component:** `AIAgentInteractive`
**Style:** A floating "Glass" container on the left or center. 

* **The Avatar:** A minimalist 3D holographic orb or a high-end 3D character (Spline) that reacts to mouse movement.
* **The Interaction:** A chat interface where the user can say: "I want to book a call for an AI Agent project."
* **The Logic:**
    1. AI asks 3 qualifying questions (Niche, Budget, Timeline).
    2. AI provides a direct [Book Meeting] link if qualified.
    3. AI summarizes the chat and sends it to your Resend inbox.

---

## 3. The Discovery Form (Dynamic Multi-Step)
**Component:** `StepByStepForm`
**Style:** High-contrast 1px black borders. Progress bar at the top in Orange.

* **Step 01: The Identity:** [Name] [Company] [Email]
* **Step 02: The Challenge:** (Dropdown) AI Agent / Workflow / Web System / Other
* **Step 03: The Vision:** [Text area for project details]
* **Button:** [Transmit Brief] (Triggers Resend Email)

---

## 4. Direct Scheduling (The Shortcut)
**Component:** `CalendarEmbed`
**Style:** Clean, white-labeled calendar (Cal.com or Calendly integration).

* **Headline:** Prefer a Direct Bridge?
* **Sub-headline:** Select a 15-minute technical discovery slot directly on our calendar.
* **Component:** [Embedded Calendar Widget]

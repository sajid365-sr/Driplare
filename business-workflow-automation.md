## 7. FAQ Section
**Component:** `AccordionFAQ`

* **Q: Is my data secure during automation?**
    * **A:** Absolutely. We prioritize end-to-end encryption and often use self-hosted tools like n8n for clients who require maximum data privacy.
* **Q: What if an automation breaks?**
    * **A:** We build "Error Handling" into every workflow. If a connection fails, you (and we) get an instant alert, and the system attempts a graceful recovery.
* **Q: Can you automate legacy software with no API?**
    * **A:** Often, yes. We use various "hooks" and custom scripts to bridge even the most stubborn software into a modern workflow.

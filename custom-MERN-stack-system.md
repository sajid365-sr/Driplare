## 6. FAQ Section
**Component:** `AccordionFAQ`

* **Q: Why choose MERN over WordPress or No-Code?**
    * **A:** MERN is for businesses that have outgrown "off-the-shelf" solutions. It offers total freedom, better security, and significantly higher performance for complex data tasks.
* **Q: How do you handle security?**
    * **A:** We implement industry-standard JWT authentication, data encryption, and secure API practices to ensure your business data remains your business data.
* **Q: Can you build mobile apps with this stack?**
    * **A:** Yes. Using React Native, we can leverage your MERN backend to launch native iOS and Android apps with shared logic, saving you time and money.

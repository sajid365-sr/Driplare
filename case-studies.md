## 3. The Case Study Grid
**Component:** `CaseStudyCard`
**Style:** A large 2-column card for each major project.

### **[Project Card: Competitor Pricing Monitor]**
* **Visual (Left):** A high-contrast "Schematic View" of the scraper logic or a dashboard screenshot with orange accents.
* **Data (Right):**
    * **Title:** Competitor Pricing & Market Intelligence System
    * **Context:** E-commerce Enterprise (5,000+ SKUs)
    * **The Problem:** 40+ manual hours per week spent tracking competitor shifts across 15 websites.
    * **The Solution:** An autonomous Node.js scraper integrated with an AI Analysis Agent and a React Dashboard.
    * **The Result (Orange Highlight):** **98% Reduction in Manual Labor | Instant Price-Adjustment Alerts.**
* **Tech Tags:** `Node.js` `n8n` `Puppeteer` `OpenAI`
